presented as a pavilion under the wrong biennale's 2o25/26 theme artificial intelligence, anything but this brings together fully human-made works created under the influence of ai. works in this pavilion present presence through absence, with gestures that manually reference the algorithmic aesthetics, or engage conceptually with the implications of ai on themes including labor, training, companionship, language, authorship, and many others.

this exhibition is curated by emily d'achiardi and jiaqi liu, featuring work by dirk sorge, josh sender, kenji kojima, kimberly lyle, liang he, louis wuttke, matthias moos, minrui qiao, nicolas crocetti, peter william holden, qinyu echo huan, scott groeniger, and yao zhang.
